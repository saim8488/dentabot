# Cosmetic Dentistry Options

## What Is Cosmetic Dentistry?

Cosmetic dentistry encompasses a range of dental procedures aimed at improving the appearance of your teeth, gums, and smile. While many cosmetic treatments also provide functional benefits, their primary goal is to enhance aesthetics.

## Popular Cosmetic Treatments

- **Teeth Whitening:** Professional bleaching to remove stains and brighten your smile.
- **Porcelain Veneers:** Thin shells bonded to the front of teeth to correct chips, gaps, discoloration, or misalignment.
- **Dental Bonding:** Tooth-colored composite resin applied to repair chips, cracks, or gaps.
- **Smile Makeovers:** A comprehensive treatment plan combining multiple cosmetic procedures for a complete smile transformation.
- **Gum Contouring:** Reshaping the gumline to create a more balanced and symmetrical smile.

## Benefits of Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic treatments can boost your self-confidence, improve your appearance, and enhance oral function. Many procedures are minimally invasive and can be completed in just one or two visits.

## Consultation and Planning

Every cosmetic treatment begins with a thorough consultation. Your dentist will discuss your goals, evaluate your oral health, and present a personalized treatment plan with expected outcomes and costs. Digital smile design tools may be used to preview your results before treatment begins.
