# First Visit Expectations

## What to Expect at Your First Visit

Your first visit to our clinic is an important step toward achieving and maintaining great oral health. Here is what you can expect so you feel prepared and comfortable.

## Before You Arrive

- Complete any new patient registration forms online or plan to arrive 15 minutes early.
- Bring your photo ID, insurance card, and a list of current medications and allergies.
- Write down any dental concerns, symptoms, or questions you would like to discuss.

## During Your Visit

1. **Welcome and Check-in:** Our front desk team will greet you, verify your information, and walk you through any remaining paperwork.
2. **Comprehensive Examination:** Your dentist will perform a thorough examination of your teeth, gums, jaw, and oral tissues.
3. **X-rays:** Digital X-rays may be taken to assess areas not visible during the clinical exam.
4. **Discussion:** Your dentist will discuss findings with you, answer your questions, and outline a personalized treatment plan if needed.
5. **Cleaning:** If time permits and no urgent issues are found, a professional cleaning may be performed during the same visit.

## After Your Visit

You will receive a summary of your exam findings, recommended treatments, and a cost estimate. Our team will help you schedule follow-up appointments as needed.

## Duration

A first visit typically takes 60 to 90 minutes. We take the time to get to know you and your dental health thoroughly.
