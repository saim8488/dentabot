# How to Ask DentaBot for Help

## What Is DentaBot?

DentaBot is our dental clinic's virtual assistant chatbot. It is available 24/7 to help you with common questions and tasks related to your dental care. Whether you need information about our services, want to book an appointment, or have a quick question, DentaBot is here to help.

## What DentaBot Can Help With

- **Appointment Booking:** Schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments.
- **Clinic Information:** Get details about our location, opening hours, and contact information.
- **Service Information:** Learn about the dental treatments and procedures we offer.
- **Insurance Questions:** Find out which insurance plans we accept and get basic billing information.
- **Oral Health Tips:** Receive advice on brushing, flossing, diet, and preventive care.
- **Emergency Guidance:** Get instructions on what to do in a dental emergency.
- **Pre-Visit Preparation:** Learn what to expect at your first visit or how to prepare for a specific procedure.

## How to Use DentaBot

- Type your question or request in plain, simple language.
- Be specific about what you need. For example: "I want to book a cleaning appointment for next Tuesday" or "What are your opening hours?"
- DentaBot will respond with relevant information or guide you through the next steps.
- If DentaBot cannot answer your question, it will offer to connect you with a human team member.

## Tips for Best Results

- Use short, clear sentences.
- Ask one question at a time for the most accurate response.
- If you are not satisfied with an answer, try rephrasing your question.
- For complex medical questions or urgent concerns, always speak with a dentist directly.

## Privacy

DentaBot does not store personal health information from your conversations. For account-specific requests (such as viewing your treatment history), you will be directed to the secure patient portal or asked to contact the front desk.
