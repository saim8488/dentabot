# Appointment Booking Process

## How to Book an Appointment

Patients can schedule appointments through several convenient methods:

- **Online Booking:** Visit our website and use the online appointment scheduler. Select your preferred date, time, and type of service.
- **Phone:** Call our front desk at (555) 123-4567 during business hours. Our reception team will find a suitable slot for you.
- **In-Person:** Walk-in patients are welcome, though we recommend booking in advance to minimize wait times.
- **DentaBot:** Use our dental chatbot assistant to book, reschedule, or inquire about available appointment times.

## What You Need to Book

When scheduling, please have the following ready: your full name, contact number, date of birth, insurance details (if applicable), and a brief description of the reason for your visit.

## Confirmation and Reminders

After booking, you will receive a confirmation via email or SMS. We send appointment reminders 48 hours and 24 hours before your scheduled visit. Please confirm your attendance by replying to the reminder message.

## Cancellation Policy

If you need to cancel or reschedule, please do so at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a small fee. We understand emergencies happen and will handle each case with flexibility.
