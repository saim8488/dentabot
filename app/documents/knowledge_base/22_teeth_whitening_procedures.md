# Teeth Whitening Procedures

## Overview

Professional teeth whitening is one of the most popular cosmetic dental treatments. It effectively removes stains and discoloration caused by coffee, tea, red wine, tobacco, aging, and certain medications, resulting in a brighter, more youthful smile.

## In-Office Whitening

In-office whitening delivers fast results in a single visit, typically lasting 60 to 90 minutes. A high-concentration bleaching gel is applied to the teeth and may be activated with a special light or laser. Gums and soft tissues are protected with a barrier during the procedure.

## Take-Home Whitening Kits

For patients who prefer to whiten at home, we provide custom-fitted whitening trays and professional-grade whitening gel. The trays are worn for a specified period each day (usually 30 minutes to an hour) over the course of one to two weeks.

## What to Expect

- Teeth may lighten several shades in a single treatment.
- Some patients experience temporary tooth sensitivity, which typically resolves within a few days.
- Results can last from several months to a few years depending on diet and oral care habits.

## Maintaining Your Results

Avoid staining foods and beverages immediately after treatment. Brush twice daily, floss regularly, and schedule periodic touch-up treatments to maintain your bright smile. Over-the-counter whitening products can supplement professional treatment but should be used as directed.
