# How to Reschedule or Cancel Appointments

## Rescheduling an Appointment

Life happens, and we understand that plans change. You can reschedule your appointment using any of the following methods:

- **Phone:** Call our front desk at (555) 123-4567 during business hours.
- **Online:** Log into your patient portal on our website and select a new date and time.
- **DentaBot:** Use our chatbot assistant to find available slots and reschedule instantly.
- **Email:** Send a rescheduling request to appointments@dentalclinic.com with your name, current appointment date, and preferred new date.

## Cancelling an Appointment

If you need to cancel, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance using any of the methods listed above. This allows us to offer the time slot to another patient in need.

## Cancellation Policy

- Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance incur no fee.
- Late cancellations (less than 24 hours' notice) or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee.
- Repeated no-shows may require a deposit for future bookings.
- We understand that genuine emergencies arise and will handle such situations with flexibility and understanding.

## Appointment Reminders

We send automated reminders via email and SMS 48 hours and 24 hours before your scheduled appointment. Please confirm or cancel through the reminder message to help us manage our schedule efficiently.

## Need Help?

If you have trouble rescheduling online or have a special request, our front desk team is happy to assist. You can also ask DentaBot for help at any time.
