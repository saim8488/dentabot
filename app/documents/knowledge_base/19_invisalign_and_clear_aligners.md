# Invisalign and Clear Aligners

## What Are Clear Aligners?

Invisalign and other clear aligner systems use a series of custom-made, transparent plastic trays to gradually shift teeth into the desired position. They offer a discreet and comfortable alternative to traditional braces.

## How Invisalign Works

1. **Consultation:** Your dentist evaluates your teeth and determines if clear aligners are suitable for your case.
2. **Digital Scan:** A 3D scan of your teeth is taken to create a precise digital model and treatment plan.
3. **Custom Aligners:** A series of aligner trays is manufactured, each designed to make incremental adjustments to tooth position.
4. **Wear Schedule:** Each set of aligners is worn for 1 to 2 weeks, approximately 20 to 22 hours per day, before switching to the next set.
5. **Progress Checks:** Regular appointments (every 6 to 8 weeks) monitor progress and provide new aligner sets.

## Advantages of Clear Aligners

- Nearly invisible when worn
- Removable for eating, drinking, brushing, and flossing
- No dietary restrictions
- Smooth plastic is more comfortable than metal brackets and wires
- Fewer office visits compared to traditional braces

## Who Is a Good Candidate?

Clear aligners work well for mild to moderate orthodontic issues, including gaps, crowding, and certain bite problems. Severe cases may still require traditional braces. Your dentist will advise on the best option for your needs.
