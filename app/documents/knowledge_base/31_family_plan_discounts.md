# Family Plan Discounts

## Our Family Dental Plan

We understand that dental care for the whole family can add up. That is why we offer special family plan discounts to help make quality dental care more affordable for households with multiple members.

## How the Family Plan Works

- Families with three or more members receiving treatment at our clinic qualify for discounted rates on preventive services.
- The discount applies to routine check-ups, professional cleanings, fluoride treatments, and dental sealants.
- Each additional family member beyond the third receives an increasing discount on eligible services.

## Eligibility

- All family members must be registered patients at our clinic.
- Family members include spouses, children, and dependents living at the same address.
- The family plan can be combined with insurance benefits where applicable.

## How to Enroll

To enroll in the family plan, speak with our front desk during your next visit or call us at (555) 123-4567. Our team will review your family's needs and apply the appropriate discounts to your accounts.

## Additional Savings

In addition to the family plan, we periodically offer seasonal promotions on teeth whitening, orthodontic consultations, and other services. Ask our staff or check our website for current offers.
