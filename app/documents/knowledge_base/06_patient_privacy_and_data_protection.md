# Patient Privacy and Data Protection

## Our Privacy Commitment

Protecting your personal and medical information is a top priority at our clinic. We comply with all applicable data protection laws and healthcare privacy regulations, including HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act).

## What Information We Collect

We collect personal details (name, address, contact information), medical and dental history, insurance information, and payment records. This data is used solely for providing dental care, processing insurance claims, and communicating with you about your treatment.

## How We Protect Your Data

- **Secure Systems:** All patient records are stored in encrypted electronic health record (EHR) systems with role-based access controls.
- **Physical Security:** Paper records, if any, are kept in locked cabinets accessible only to authorized staff.
- **Staff Training:** All team members receive regular training on data privacy and confidentiality protocols.

## Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be shared only with your insurance provider for claims processing, with referring specialists as needed, or when required by law.

## Your Rights

You have the right to access your dental records, request corrections, and ask how your data is being used. To make a privacy-related request, contact our front desk or email our privacy officer at privacy@dentalclinic.com.
