# Clinic Location and Directions

## Address

Our dental clinic is conveniently located at 123 Smile Avenue, Downtown Health District, Suite 200. We are situated near the central business area, making it easy to visit before or after work.

## How to Find Us

- **By Car:** From the main highway, take Exit 12 toward Downtown. Turn right on Smile Avenue and our building is on the left. Free parking is available in the lot behind the building.
- **By Public Transit:** The nearest bus stop is Smile Avenue Station, served by Routes 5, 12, and 18. The Downtown Metro Station is a 5-minute walk from our clinic.
- **By Foot:** We are located next to the Central Park Pharmacy and across from Green Valley Bank, both well-known local landmarks.

## Parking Information

We offer complimentary parking for all patients in our rear parking lot. Accessible parking spaces are available near the building entrance. Street parking with metered rates is also available along Smile Avenue.

## Accessibility

Our clinic is fully wheelchair accessible. The building features ramp access at the front entrance, an elevator to the second floor, and accessible restrooms. If you need any special accommodations, please let us know when booking your appointment.
