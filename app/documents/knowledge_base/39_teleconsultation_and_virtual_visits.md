# Teleconsultation and Virtual Visits

## What Is a Dental Teleconsultation?

A dental teleconsultation is a virtual appointment conducted via video call, allowing you to consult with a dentist from the comfort of your home. It is a convenient option for initial assessments, follow-up consultations, and non-emergency dental advice.

## When to Use Teleconsultation

- Discussing dental concerns or symptoms before scheduling an in-person visit
- Post-treatment follow-ups to check on healing progress
- Reviewing treatment plans and asking questions
- Getting advice on minor dental issues such as sensitivity, mouth sores, or oral hygiene
- Prescription renewals for dental medications

## How It Works

1. **Book Online:** Schedule a virtual visit through our website or by calling the front desk.
2. **Receive a Link:** You will receive a secure video call link via email or SMS before your appointment.
3. **Join the Call:** At the scheduled time, click the link to connect with your dentist. Have good lighting and be prepared to show the area of concern using your phone or webcam.
4. **Consultation:** The dentist will assess your concern, provide recommendations, and schedule an in-person visit if needed.

## Limitations

Teleconsultations are not suitable for dental emergencies, procedures requiring physical examination, or treatments that need X-rays or hands-on care. In such cases, an in-person visit will be recommended.

## Cost and Insurance

Teleconsultation fees are typically lower than in-person visits. Many insurance plans now cover virtual dental visits. Contact our office to verify coverage.
