# Patient Comfort Amenities

## Your Comfort Is Our Priority

We strive to make every visit as pleasant and stress-free as possible. Our clinic is designed with patient comfort in mind, from the waiting room to the treatment chair.

## Amenities We Offer

- **Comfortable Waiting Area:** Enjoy a clean, modern waiting room with comfortable seating, complimentary Wi-Fi, and a selection of magazines and reading materials.
- **Beverages:** Complimentary water, coffee, and tea are available in the waiting area.
- **Entertainment:** TVs are mounted in treatment rooms so you can watch your favorite shows during procedures. Noise-canceling headphones are available upon request.
- **Blankets and Pillows:** Cozy blankets and neck pillows are available to help you relax during treatment.
- **Aromatherapy:** Calming scents are used throughout the clinic to create a soothing atmosphere.

## For Children

Our kid-friendly area includes toys, coloring books, and a small play zone to keep young patients entertained while they wait.

## Special Requests

If there is anything that would make your visit more comfortable—a specific room temperature, a break during a long procedure, or extra explanation of what is happening—please let our team know. We are here to help you feel at ease.
