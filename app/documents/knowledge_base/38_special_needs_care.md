# Special Needs Care

## Inclusive Dental Care

Our clinic is committed to providing compassionate, high-quality dental care to patients with special needs, including those with physical, developmental, intellectual, sensory, or behavioral conditions.

## Our Approach

- **Individualized Treatment Plans:** We tailor treatment to each patient's abilities, comfort level, and medical requirements.
- **Trained Staff:** Our dental team has specialized training in caring for patients with special needs, including communication techniques and behavioral management.
- **Flexible Scheduling:** We offer longer appointment times, quiet hours, and reduced-stimulation environments to accommodate patients who may need a calmer setting.
- **Caregiver Involvement:** Family members and caregivers are welcome in the treatment room to provide support and comfort.

## Sedation Options

For patients who have difficulty tolerating dental procedures, we offer nitrous oxide, oral sedation, and IV sedation options. Our team will discuss the safest and most appropriate sedation approach based on the patient's medical history.

## Accessibility

Our facility is fully wheelchair accessible with wide doorways, adjustable treatment chairs, and accessible restrooms. We can accommodate patients who use mobility devices or require transfer assistance.

## Scheduling a Visit

When booking an appointment for a patient with special needs, please inform our front desk of any specific accommodations or medical considerations. This allows us to prepare appropriately and ensure the best possible experience.
