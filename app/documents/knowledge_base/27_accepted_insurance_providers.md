# Accepted Insurance Providers

## Insurance We Accept

Our clinic works with a wide range of dental insurance providers to make quality dental care affordable and accessible. We accept plans from the following major insurance companies (this list is not exhaustive):

- Delta Dental
- Cigna Dental
- Aetna Dental
- MetLife Dental
- UnitedHealthcare Dental
- Guardian Dental
- Humana Dental
- Blue Cross Blue Shield Dental
- Principal Financial Group
- Ameritas

## Verifying Your Coverage

We recommend contacting your insurance company or our front desk before your appointment to verify your coverage, copays, and any plan limitations. Our billing team can help verify your benefits and explain what is covered under your plan.

## In-Network vs. Out-of-Network

We are in-network with most major dental plans. If we are not in your plan's network, we can still provide care, and we will assist you in submitting out-of-network claims for potential reimbursement.

## Questions About Insurance

If you are unsure whether your insurance is accepted, please call us at (555) 123-4567 or ask DentaBot for assistance. We are happy to help you understand your dental benefits and maximize your coverage.
