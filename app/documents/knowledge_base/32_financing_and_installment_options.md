# Financing and Installment Options

## Affordable Dental Care

We believe that cost should not be a barrier to receiving necessary dental treatment. For patients who need help managing larger treatment costs, we offer several financing and installment options.

## In-House Payment Plans

For qualifying treatments, we offer interest-free in-house payment plans. You can split the cost of your treatment into manageable monthly installments over a period of 3 to 12 months. A down payment may be required at the start of treatment.

## Third-Party Financing

We partner with reputable third-party financing companies to provide additional payment flexibility:

- **CareCredit:** Offers promotional financing with low or no interest for qualified applicants. Apply online or at our clinic.
- **LendingClub:** Provides fixed-rate personal loans for dental procedures with predictable monthly payments.
- **Sunbit:** Offers easy approval for payment plans with no hard credit check.

## How to Apply

Ask our billing team for details on available financing options. Applications can be completed at the clinic or online. Approval decisions are typically provided within minutes.

## Planning for Major Treatments

If you need extensive dental work such as implants, orthodontics, or full-mouth restoration, we encourage you to discuss financing early in the treatment planning process. Our team will help you find a payment solution that fits your budget.
