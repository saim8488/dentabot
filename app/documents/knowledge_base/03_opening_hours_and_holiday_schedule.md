## Extended Hours

On Thursdays, we offer extended evening hours until 7:00 PM to accommodate patients with busy work schedules. Saturday appointments are available for routine check-ups and urgent care needs.

## Holiday Closures

Our clinic is closed on the following public holidays: New Year's Day, Independence Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day. We may also have reduced hours on the eve of major holidays. Please check our website or call the front desk for the most up-to-date holiday schedule.

## Emergency After-Hours Contact

If you experience a dental emergency outside of regular hours, please call our emergency hotline at (555) 123-4567. A member of our on-call team will guide you on the next steps.
