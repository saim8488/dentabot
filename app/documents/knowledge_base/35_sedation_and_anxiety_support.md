# Sedation and Anxiety Support

## Dental Anxiety Is Common

Many people experience anxiety or fear about dental visits. Our clinic offers several sedation and comfort options to help anxious patients receive the care they need in a calm and relaxed state.

## Sedation Options

- **Nitrous Oxide (Laughing Gas):** A mild sedative inhaled through a small mask placed over the nose. It helps you relax while remaining fully conscious. Effects wear off quickly after the mask is removed.
- **Oral Sedation:** A prescribed sedative pill taken before your appointment produces a moderate level of relaxation. You will be awake but drowsy. A companion must drive you home after your visit.
- **IV Sedation:** Administered intravenously for a deeper level of sedation. Ideal for longer or more complex procedures and patients with severe anxiety. You will have little to no memory of the procedure.

## Who Can Benefit?

Sedation dentistry is suitable for patients who:
- Experience dental phobia or severe anxiety
- Have a strong gag reflex
- Need extensive dental work completed in one visit
- Have difficulty getting numb with local anesthesia alone
- Have special needs that make it hard to sit still during treatment

## Safety and Monitoring

All sedation is administered by trained professionals. Vital signs including heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen levels are monitored throughout the procedure. A thorough medical history review is conducted before any sedation is provided.

## Talk to Us

If dental anxiety is keeping you from getting the care you need, please let us know. We will work with you to find the most comfortable approach for your treatment.
