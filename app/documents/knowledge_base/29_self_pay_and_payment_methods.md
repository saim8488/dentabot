# Self-Pay and Payment Methods

## Payment Options for Uninsured Patients

We believe everyone deserves access to quality dental care, regardless of insurance status. Self-pay patients are welcome at our clinic, and we offer transparent pricing and flexible payment options.

## Accepted Payment Methods

- Cash
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards
- Personal checks
- Health Savings Account (HSA) and Flexible Spending Account (FSA) cards
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

## Self-Pay Discounts

Uninsured patients may be eligible for a courtesy discount when paying in full at the time of service. Ask our front desk about current self-pay pricing and any available promotional offers.

## Payment at Time of Service

For most procedures, payment is expected at the time of service. For larger treatments, we can provide a detailed cost estimate in advance so there are no surprises. Payment plans and financing options are also available for more extensive treatment.

## Questions About Billing

If you have questions about your bill or need to discuss payment arrangements, please contact our billing department at (555) 123-4567 or speak to our front desk staff. We are committed to working with you to make dental care affordable.
