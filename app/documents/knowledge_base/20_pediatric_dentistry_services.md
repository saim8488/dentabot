# Pediatric Dentistry Services

## What Is Pediatric Dentistry?

Pediatric dentistry focuses on the oral health of infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatric dental team is trained to address the unique dental needs of young patients in a friendly, supportive, and fun environment.

## Services for Children

- **First Dental Visit:** Recommended by age 1 or within six months of the first tooth erupting.
- **Routine Exams and Cleanings:** Regular check-ups to monitor tooth development and maintain oral health.
- **Fluoride Treatments:** Strengthen developing teeth and help prevent cavities.
- **Dental Sealants:** Thin protective coatings applied to the chewing surfaces of back teeth to prevent decay.
- **Cavity Treatment:** Gentle fillings and restorations designed for young patients.
- **Orthodontic Evaluation:** Early assessment of bite and alignment issues.

## Child-Friendly Environment

Our clinic features a kid-friendly waiting area with books, toys, and games. Treatment rooms are designed to make children feel comfortable and at ease. Our team uses age-appropriate language to explain procedures and reduce anxiety.

## Tips for Parents

- Start brushing your child's teeth as soon as the first tooth appears.
- Supervise brushing until your child is around age 7 to ensure proper technique.
- Limit sugary snacks and drinks.
- Make dental visits a positive experience by using encouraging language at home.
