# New Patient Registration Process

## Welcome to Our Clinic

We are delighted to welcome new patients. Our registration process is straightforward and designed to get you started with minimal hassle.

## How to Register

- **Online:** Complete the new patient registration form on our website before your first visit. This saves time at the clinic.
- **In-Person:** Arrive 15 minutes early for your first appointment to complete the paperwork at the front desk.
- **Required Documents:** Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and a list of any current medications or known allergies.

## Medical and Dental History

As part of registration, you will be asked to provide your medical and dental history. This includes any prior surgeries, chronic conditions, medications, and previous dental treatments. Accurate information helps our dentists provide safe and effective care.

## Consent Forms

New patients must review and sign consent forms covering treatment authorization, privacy policies, and financial responsibility. Our staff will walk you through each form and answer any questions.

## What Happens Next

After registration, you will be assigned to a dental care team. Your first visit typically includes a comprehensive oral examination, X-rays if needed, and a discussion of your dental health goals and treatment options.
