# Insurance Claim Process

## How Insurance Claims Work

When you receive dental treatment at our clinic, we handle the insurance claim process on your behalf to make the experience as smooth as possible. Our billing team submits claims directly to your insurance provider.

## Steps in the Claim Process

1. **Verification:** Before your appointment, we verify your insurance eligibility and benefits.
2. **Treatment:** You receive dental care. Your copay or estimated out-of-pocket cost is collected at the time of service.
3. **Claim Submission:** Our billing team submits the claim electronically to your insurance company, typically within 1 to 2 business days after your visit.
4. **Processing:** The insurance company reviews the claim and determines the covered amount. This usually takes 2 to 4 weeks.
5. **Explanation of Benefits (EOB):** You will receive an EOB from your insurer detailing what was covered and any remaining balance.
6. **Balance Billing:** If there is any remaining balance after insurance payment, we will send you a statement.

## Pre-Authorization

For certain procedures (such as crowns, implants, or orthodontics), pre-authorization from your insurance company may be required. Our team will submit the necessary documentation and obtain approval before treatment begins.

## Assistance with Claims

If you have questions about a claim, denial, or your EOB, our billing department is available to assist. We can help appeal denied claims and clarify coverage details.
