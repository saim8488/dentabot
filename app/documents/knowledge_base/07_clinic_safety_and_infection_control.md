# Clinic Safety and Infection Control

## Our Commitment to Safety

Patient and staff safety is fundamental to our practice. We maintain rigorous infection control protocols that meet or exceed national dental safety guidelines and standards set by the CDC and OSHA.

## Sterilization Procedures

- All reusable dental instruments are cleaned, disinfected, and sterilized using hospital-grade autoclaves after every use.
- Sterilization equipment is tested regularly with biological indicators to ensure effectiveness.
- Single-use items such as gloves, needles, and suction tips are disposed of immediately after use in designated biohazard containers.

## Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

Our dental team wears appropriate PPE during all procedures, including gloves, masks, protective eyewear, and gowns. PPE is changed between patients to prevent cross-contamination.

## Surface Disinfection and Air Quality

Treatment rooms are thoroughly disinfected between patients, including all surfaces, chairs, and equipment handles. We use medical-grade disinfectants and maintain high-efficiency air filtration systems to ensure clean air throughout the clinic.

## Patient Safety Measures

Patients are provided with protective eyewear and bibs during treatments. We screen for health conditions and allergies before every procedure. Hand sanitizer stations are available throughout the clinic for patient and visitor use.
